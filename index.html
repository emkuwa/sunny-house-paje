About Our Home

Welcome to our cozy home in the heart of Paje village, Zanzibar! Sunny House Paje is a small local guesthouse that offers a warm and inviting atmosphere perfect for travelers seeking a simple and authentic Zanzibari experience.

Our house is owned and managed by Sarah and Axel, who are passionate about making your stay comfortable and memorable. With their extensive knowledge of the area, they're always ready to provide recommendations and assist with organizing activities.

Why Choose Sunny House Paje?

  • Less than a minute walk to the stunning Paje Beach
  • Affordable accommodation options for various budgets
  • Friendly, knowledgeable hosts who ensure a pleasant stay
  • Close to kitesurfing spots and water activities
  • Access to shared kitchen and garden relaxation areas

Activities

Kitesurfing

Paje is renowned as a world-class kitesurfing destination. With consistent winds, shallow waters, and several professional schools nearby, it's perfect for beginners and experienced kitesurfers alike.

Multiple kitesurfing schools within walking distance

Beach Relaxation

Enjoy the pristine white sand beaches and crystal-clear turquoise waters just steps from our property. Perfect for sunbathing, swimming, and watching stunning sunrises.

Beach bars and restaurants with ocean views nearby

Snorkeling & Diving

Discover the vibrant marine life in Zanzibar's coral reefs. Snorkeling trips and diving excursions can be arranged to explore the underwater wonders.

Our hosts can help arrange tours with reliable operators

Cultural Experiences

Immerse yourself in local Zanzibari culture by visiting nearby villages, markets, and historical sites. Experience the friendly hospitality of the local people.

Local markets and community interactions available

Stone Town Excursion

Visit the UNESCO World Heritage site of Stone Town with its narrow alleys, historical buildings, and vibrant markets. A day trip from Paje brings you to this cultural treasure.

Transportation can be arranged through our hosts

Jozani Forest

Explore the Jozani Forest, home to the rare Red Colobus Monkeys. This natural reserve is just 12 miles from Sunny House Paje and offers a glimpse into Zanzibar's diverse ecosystem.

Half-day tours available with expert guides
